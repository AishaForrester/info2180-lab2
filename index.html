Building from the ashes

Be your own motivator

September 30, 2023 by Aisha Forrester

Life is a canvas left to our imagination. Sometimes we feel the need to peer on the painting of our neighbour. Admittedly, sometimes their art may seem more unique and even more captivating than our own. Sometimes we feel that our art is one that is meant to be scrutinized and thrown back into our faces if they do not fit a certain societal construct. We force ourselves into believing that we must be the best. But what makes a better artist? Is it not the same unrefined authenticity that we rob ourselves of?

Sometimes we need to take a step back and make use of the resources that were designed to create our very own masterpiece. We must motivate ourselves to believe that we contain all that is necessary to succeed.

The Importance of education

October 1, 2023 by Aisha Forrester

School plays a fundamental role in shaping the future of individuals and society as a whole. It serves as the cornerstone of education, equipping students with knowledge, skills, and critical thinking abilities that are essential for personal growth and success in the ever-evolving world. In the classroom, students not only gain subject- specific knowledge but also cultivate essential life skills such as communication, problem-solving, and teamwork.

Furthermore, schools play a vital role in promoting equality and inclusivity in society. They provide an opportunity for individuals from diverse backgrounds to access education and create a level playing field.
